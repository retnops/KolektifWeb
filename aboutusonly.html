Company Background

PT. Graha Unika Sejahtera (GUS) was incorporated in 2003, specializing in providing innovative IT solution with cutting edge of technology.

Vision

To become the leading IT Solution provider globally by combining the mastery of the state-of-the-art IT technology with a deep understanding of bussiness needs, in order to enforce automated and paperless operations in any business entity.

Mission

To provide Information Technology solution that truly works to meet - even exceed - expectations of clients to improve business performance, speed-up sustainable growth and business expansion.

Timeline

Some facts about us

  • 2003

    Start Implement Various Telco Solutions

    Combining cutting edge of technology and deep understanding of local environment and needs, GUS has successfully provided innovative IT solution in Indonesia

    Starting with SCM & CRM System solutions, GUS also became the first and (until today) the only Indonesia company that develop and implement the "core solution" for the telecommunication industry

  • 2005

    Implement Service Delivery Platform for Telco Operators

    GUS deployed the SDP(Service Delivery Platform) in TELKOM-Flexi and Bakrie Telecom since 2005, enabling both operators to implement the Just-in-Time Service Provisioning scenario. In the years that followed, several well-known services such as Flexi-Kiosk, Flexi-COMBO, and Esia-GOGO was developed on top of the SDP

  • 2010

    Focus in Mobility Solution

    With the hype of smartphone, GUS shifted its focus on mobility solution in 2010

  • 2011

    mWA: Telkomsel (>5000 users)

    By 2011, GUS implemented Mobile Workforce Automation(mWA) in Telkomsel for more than 5,000 active users nationwide. The success was followed by winning international bid to implement mWA in Indosat(>3500 active users) and XL-Axiata(>3000 active users) respectively. mWA also successfully implemented in BFI Finance

  • 2012

    mWA: Indosat (> 3500 users)

    The success of mWA was followed by winning international bid to implement mWA in Indosat(>3500 active users) and XL-Axiata(>3000 active users) respectively. mWA also successfully implemented in BFI Finance

  • 2014

    MO: Indosat (>500.000 users)

    Mobile Outlet(MO) successfully deployed for more than 500,000 Indosat Retail POS across Indonesia in 2014

  • 2015

    mWA: XL Axiata (>3000 users)

    mWA XL-Axiata reached more than 3000 active users.

  • 2016

    MO: XL Axiata (>300.000 users)

    MO implementation in XL-Axiata (> 300,000 active users) and Telkomsel(>1,000,000 active users)

    KREN Talk & KREN Pay Fully released

    In 2016, KREN Talk (Next Generation UCC) and KREN Pay (New Generation of Digital POS & Wallet) started to roll.

  • 2017

    MO: Telkomsel (>1.000.000 users)
